HS-Brexit ACTOR Random Acquisition Round 2
==========================================

Dataset:
HS-Brexit

Model:
ACTOR (shared bert-base-uncased encoder with annotator-specific heads)

Architecture:
- Shared BERT encoder
- 6 annotator-specific linear heads
- CLS embedding used as sentence representation
- Each example routed through the head corresponding to its annotator

Experiment type:
Active learning setup - random acquisition, round 2

Training data:
Round-2 labeled pool after adding 200 more randomly acquired examples

Labeled pool size:
1000 annotation-level examples

Dev/Test sets:
Original annotation-level dev and test sets

Training setup:
epochs: 3
batch size: 8
learning rate: 2e-5
weight decay: 0.01
evaluation metric for model selection: macro_f1

Round-2 pool label distribution:
0 (non-hate): 877
1 (hate): 123

Test results:
eval_loss: 0.3417
eval_accuracy: 0.8810
eval_macro_f1: 0.5731
eval_weighted_f1: 0.8407

Notes:
This experiment trains ACTOR after two random acquisition rounds.

Comparison:
- Initial pool ACTOR macro-F1: 0.4650
- Random round 1 ACTOR macro-F1: 0.6107
- Random round 2 ACTOR macro-F1: 0.5731

The round-2 score did not surpass round 1, which is possible under random
acquisition because newly sampled examples are not guaranteed to be maximally
informative.

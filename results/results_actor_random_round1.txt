HS-Brexit ACTOR Random Acquisition Round 1
==========================================

Dataset:
HS-Brexit

Model:
ACTOR (shared bert-base-uncased encoder with annotator-specific heads)

Architecture:
- Shared BERT encoder
- 6 annotator-specific linear heads
- CLS embedding used as sentence representation
- Each example routed through the head corresponding to its annotator

Experiment type:
Active learning setup - random acquisition, round 1

Training data:
Round-1 labeled pool after adding 200 randomly acquired examples

Labeled pool size:
800 annotation-level examples

Dev/Test sets:
Original annotation-level dev and test sets

Training setup:
epochs: 3
batch size: 8
learning rate: 2e-5
weight decay: 0.01
evaluation metric for model selection: macro_f1

Round-1 pool label distribution:
0 (non-hate): 697
1 (hate): 103

Test results:
eval_loss: 0.3479
eval_accuracy: 0.8740
eval_macro_f1: 0.6107
eval_weighted_f1: 0.8470

Notes:
This experiment trains ACTOR after one random acquisition round.
Compared to the initial labeled-pool model, performance improved noticeably,
especially in macro-F1.

Comparison:
- Initial pool ACTOR macro-F1: 0.4650
- Random round 1 ACTOR macro-F1: 0.6107
